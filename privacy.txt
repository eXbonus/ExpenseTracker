PRIVACY POLICY FOR EXPENSE TRACKER PRO

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Thank you for choosing Expense Tracker Pro. Your privacy is our absolute highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

1. DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE
Expense Tracker Pro is a fully offline, local-first application. We do not collect, transmit, store, or process any of your personal or financial data on external servers. 
- All transactions, categories, and settings you enter are stored strictly locally on your device's internal storage.
- If you choose to attach images (like receipts) to your transactions, these images remain exclusively on your device.

2. NO THIRD-PARTY TRACKING OR SHARING
Because your data never leaves your device, it is never shared with us, nor is it shared with any third parties. 
- We do not use third-party analytics trackers.
- We do not use advertising frameworks that harvest user data.
- We do not sell or rent any user information (because we do not have access to it).

3. DEVICE PERMISSIONS
To provide core functionalities, the app may request certain on-device permissions:
- Storage/Files: Required strictly to allow you to import Excel/CSV files or attach receipt images to your transactions. This data is processed locally and never uploaded to the internet.

4. USER CONTROL AND DATA DELETION
You retain 100% control over your data. 
- You can delete individual transactions at any time.
- You can permanently erase all app data, including all transaction history and settings, by using the "Reset All Data" option located within the app's Settings menu. Uninstalling the app will also clear this local data.

5. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's features or legal requirements. Any changes will be updated directly within this document.
